The science of Sleep

The risk of developing depression is 5x higher in those with insomnia. Considering how a lack of sleep can negatively affect our mood the following day, it makes sense to get enough of it every day, become happier, and consequently increase our productivity at work.


Consider sleeping early for ideally 7-9 hours of sleep per day.

Effects of Exercise

Excercise increases endorphins, reduces stress, and is a proven remedy for both depression and anxiety. The mood benefits of only 20 minutes of excercise can last for 12 hours!


Exercise outside for at least 30 minutes, three to five times a week for maximum mental health benefits.
